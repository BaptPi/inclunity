The 4-Month System

A sustainable, human-centred model empowering individuals toward true independence — step by step, with dignity.

Month 1–2

Assessment & Stability

Beneficiaries live rent-free in safe housing. Comprehensive assessments of their skills, goals, and needs are conducted by our trained coordinators. E-learning access begins.

Month 3–4

Skill Building & Work

Continued rent-free living while participants engage in professional training, workshops, or part-time work placements matched to their profile and the local job market.

Post 4 Months

Reduced Rent Transition

Participants begin paying a significantly reduced rent — designed to mirror real-world conditions and gradually build financial responsibility while retaining full program support.

Goal Reached

Independence & Giving Back

Once ready to live independently, participants return the apartment — making it available for the next person on the waiting list. Many become volunteers or contributors themselves.

Common Questions

Who can apply?

Any adult facing housing insecurity or unemployment. Applications are reviewed by our local coordinators.

Is it really free?

Yes. The first 4 months are entirely rent-free. The transition phase is subsidized to remain affordable.

Where are the apartments?

We currently operate in 40+ cities across 30 countries. New locations are added every quarter.
